The Guru Granth Sahib is the central religious text of Sikhism. It is considered by Sikhs to be their eternal Guru or teacher. It contains the baani (words) and shabads (hymns) spoken and composed by the Sikh Gurus and bhagats (saints) from other religions who shared the same core message.

This is a visually minimalistic website to explore the Guru Granth Sahib.
